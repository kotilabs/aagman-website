Link your Zerodha trading account to Āagman so you can place and manage live orders by prompt. Setup has two parts — connecting through Kite Connect, then pairing the Āagman Relay.

Connecting a broker is only required to place and manage live orders from Āagman. Backtesting, screening, charts, and options analysis all work without a broker connection.

Setup has two parts — linking your Zerodha account through Kite Connect, then pairing the Āagman Relay so orders can be routed to the broker through your local machine.

Z

K

PART 01Connect your Zerodha account

Āagman talks to Zerodha through Zerodha's official Kite Connect API. You'll create a personal API app on the Kite Connect developer portal, then plug those credentials into Āagman.

01

Create an API app on Kite Connect

  1. Sign in to the Kite Connect developer portal at developers.kite.trade.
  2. Click Create a new app.
  3. For Type, choose Personal. Do not pick Connect — the Personal type is what Āagman expects for an individual trading account.
  4. Fill in the app details:
    • App name — anything you'll recognize (e.g. Āagman).
    • Redirect URL — open Āagman in another tab, go to Settings→Broker Account, and your redirect URL is listed there. Copy it and paste it in exactly as shown.
    • Description, icon, postback URL — fill in as Zerodha requires.
  5. Submit the form and complete payment if Zerodha prompts. Zerodha will then issue your API Key and API Secret.
Note

Keep the Kite Connect portal tab open — you'll need to paste the API Key and API Secret into Āagman in the next step.

02

Add your credentials to Āagman

In Āagman, go to Settings→Broker Account→Zerodha and enter the following:

  • API Key — from your Kite Connect app.
  • API Secret — from your Kite Connect app.
  • Client Code — your Zerodha trading account client ID (e.g. AB1234).
  • Account Label — any label that helps you identify this account (e.g. Primary, Spouse account, Algo testing). For your reference only.

Hit Save to store the credentials.

03

Authenticate with Zerodha

  1. On the Zerodha account screen in Āagman, click Authenticate.
  2. You'll be redirected to the Zerodha login page. Sign in with your Zerodha credentials.
  3. When prompted, authorize Āagman to access your account.
✓ Once authorization succeeds, you're returned to Āagman and your Zerodha account is connected.

PART 02Pair the Āagman Relay

The Āagman Relay is a Chrome extension that lets Āagman communicate with your broker through your local machine. It routes orders out to Zerodha and relays responses back to Āagman. The relay must be installed and paired before you can place live orders.

01

Install the Relay extension

Install the Āagman Relay from the Chrome Web Store.

Get Āagman Relay ↗

Once installed, you'll see the Āagman Relay icon in your Chrome toolbar.

02

Pair the extension with Āagman

  1. In Āagman, go to Settings→Relay Agent.
  2. Click Install and Pair.
  3. Click Pair the Extension.
  4. When Chrome prompts, authorize the requested permissions.
  5. Wait a few seconds for the handshake to complete.
✓ You'll see a confirmation in Āagman once pairing is successful.

You're set

Your broker account and relay are both connected. Head to Live to start placing and managing orders from Āagman by prompt.

Troubleshooting

If something doesn't work end-to-end, double-check the following.

  • Redirect URL match. The Redirect URL in your Kite app should match the one shown in Settings→Broker Account in Āagman, character-for-character.
  • Relay enabled and paired. The Āagman Relay extension is enabled in Chrome and shows as paired in Settings→Relay Agent.
