How to Prompt

Aagman understands natural language — describe what you want the way you'd explain it to another trader. No forms, no dropdowns. You can prompt in English, Hindi, Hinglish, Tamil, Telugu, or any language you think in.

Start Here: Your First Prompt

Here's the same idea expressed four ways — one for each thing Aagman does:

Backtest a long-only EMA crossover on RELIANCE, daily, Jan to Dec 2025. Buy when EMA(20) crosses above EMA(50). Stop loss 1.5%, take profit 4.5%. 50 shares per trade.
Find NIFTY 50 stocks where RSI(14) is above 70.
Backtest portfolio of top 3 stocks from RELIANCE, TCS, INFY, HDFCBANK ranked by momentum, monthly rebalance, 10 lakh capital, Jan 2023 to Dec 2024.
Buy 50 shares of RELIANCE at market, live. Stop-loss 1% below, target 2% above.

Different goals, same principle. Each prompt tells Aagman what instrument, what condition, what risk, and what mode. What changes is how specific you get with each piece.

The Five Pieces of a Prompt

PieceWhat Aagman needsExample
SymbolWhich stock, index, or contract — applies to trades, backtests, and screenersRELIANCE / NIFTY 50 universe / BANKNIFTY 24500 CE
Direction & sizeBuy or sell, and how much — for trades and backtests; screeners skip thisBuy 50 shares / Sell 1 lot / Use 10% of capital
Entry ruleThe condition that triggers action — price, indicator, or screener filterAt market / When RSI crosses above 30 / ROE above 15%
Exit & riskHow the position closes — for trades and backtests; screeners skip thisStop-loss 2%, target 5% / Trailing stop 3% / Exit after 5 bars
ModeBacktest, live trade, paper trade, or screenLive / Paper / Backtest Jan–Dec 2024 / Screen now

If you skip a piece, Aagman asks for it rather than guessing. The more complete your prompt, the faster you get to results.

01Deploy a Live or Paper Trade

Say "live" and Aagman places the order after confirming. Say "paper" to simulate. Specify intraday (auto-squares off at EOD) or delivery/CNC (carries overnight).

Simple bracket order

Go live: sell 100 SBIN intraday with a 0.5% stop and 1.5% take-profit.

Indicator-driven intraday

Deploy live: buy NIFTYBEES intraday when RSI(14) crosses above 30, exit when it crosses 70. 1% stop-loss.

Positional with trailing stop

Deploy live as delivery (CNC): buy 30 ITC if weekly RSI is below 40, hold with a 5% trailing stop.

Options — single leg

Buy 1 lot of NIFTY 24500 CE at market, live. Stop-loss 30%, target 60%.

Options — multi-leg straddle

Deploy a live BANKNIFTY short straddle at ATM, 1 lot each leg, combined stop-loss ₹6,000, square-off at 3:15.

Hinglish works natively

Live deploy karo — NIFTY ka RSI 30 ke neeche jaaye toh BankNifty mein bull call spread daal do. Weekly expiry, 1 lot.

02Backtest a Strategy

Same structure as a live deploy — instead of "live," give a date range. Aagman runs historically and returns a performance report.

Basic equity backtest

Backtest a long-only EMA crossover on RELIANCE, daily, Jan to Dec 2025. Go LONG when EMA(20) crosses above EMA(50). Stop loss 1.5%, take profit 4.5%. Buy 50 shares per trade.

Chained indicators

Buy SBIN when EMA(10) of RSI(14) crosses above 50, daily, Jan to Dec 2024. Exit when it crosses below 45. 100 shares. Stop loss 4%.

Exit types

Say "trailing stop at 2%" to track the high since entry. "Exit after 5 bars" for a time stop. "Use 10% of capital per trade" for percentage sizing. Combine freely.

Options backtest

Backtest a short straddle on NIFTY, Jan to Apr 2025. SELL ATM call and ATM put when INDIA VIX > 20. Monthly expiry. Close when VIX drops below 15.

03Build a Portfolio

Define a universe, tell Aagman how to rank and select, set a rebalance schedule.

Backtest portfolio of top 3 stocks from RELIANCE, TCS, INFY, HDFCBANK, ICICIBANK, SBIN, WIPRO, LT ranked by momentum score, monthly rebalance, equal weight, 10 lakh capital, Jan 2023 to Dec 2024.

Rank by momentum, Sortino ratio, period returns, or lowest volatility. Add a dynamic filter at each rebalance:

…but only include stocks where RSI(14) is below 50 at each rebalance.

Buy and hold

Equal weight portfolio of RELIANCE, TCS, INFY, HDFCBANK, ICICIBANK, no rebalancing, buy and hold, 10 lakh capital.

04Run a Screener

Simplest prompts — just describe what you're filtering for. No date range or sizing needed.

Technical

Find NIFTY 50 stocks at their 52-week high.
Find NIFTY 50 stocks where 50 SMA just crossed above 200 SMA.
Show me NIFTY 50 stocks that gapped up more than 1%.

Fundamental

Value stocks with PE below 12, price to book below 1, dividend yield above 4%.
Find stocks with promoter holding above 70% and FII above 10%.

Hybrid — Aagman's strongest

ROE > 10 stocks showing bullish MACD crossover.
High margin stocks (operating margin > 25%) near 52-week lows — price < EMA 200.

Use AND for all conditions to match, OR when any one is enough.

Tips

syntax

No perfect syntax needed. "Buy SBIN when EMA 20 crosses EMA 50" works as well as the formal version.

options

For options, BUY and SELL mean the option leg itself — not your view on the underlying.

capital

Starting capital is optional for basic backtests but needed for return percentages or percentage-based sizing.

context

A longer prompt rarely breaks anything. A missing piece sometimes does — when in doubt, be explicit.
