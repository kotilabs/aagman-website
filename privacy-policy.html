Aligned to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Data controller: Koti Labs Private Limited ("Āagman", "we").

01

Scope & consent

This policy governs personal data we process when you create an account and use Āagman. By accepting it at signup you consent to the processing described here. You may withdraw consent, which may limit the service.

02

Data we collect

Account

Name, mobile number, email, password (hashed).

Broker-connection

Broker API keys / access tokens, broker client identifiers, the static IP you whitelist. We do not store your broker login password.

Trading & usage

Strategies you build or deploy, orders, positions and fills retrieved from your broker / exchange, backtest and screener activity, in-app interactions.

Technical

Device, IP address, log and diagnostic data.

03

Purposes

Provide the service and route algo orders to your own broker account

Authenticate and secure access

Regulatory audit, order-tagging and record-keeping

Grievance handling and support

Service improvement

We do not sell your personal data.

04

Sharing & storage

We share data with your broker(s) and the exchange(s) to place and manage orders and meet algo-tagging / audit obligations; with regulators when legally required; and with vetted sub-processors under confidentiality.

Personal and order data are hosted on servers located in India.

05

Retention

Retained for the life of your account and thereafter as required by SEBI / exchange record-keeping (at least 5 years for trading and audit records), then deleted or anonymised.

06

Security

Encryption in transit, hashed credentials, OAuth and two-factor authentication, static-IP whitelisting, least-privilege access controls and audit logging.

No system is perfectly secure; report concerns to the Grievance Officer.
